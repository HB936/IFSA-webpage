Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the competition? ▼
Click the "Register on Unstop" button above to be redirected to our secure registration platform where you can complete your registration and payment.
How is the prize pool calculated? ▼
The prize pool starts with a €500 seed fund from Crowwd. Each participant adds €10, so the total prize pool grows with every new registration.
What is Crowwd? ▼
Crowwd is our trading platform partner that provides the virtual trading environment with real market data and professional-grade tools for the competition.
Is the payment process secure? ▼
Yes, all payments are processed securely through Unstop's encrypted payment system with industry-standard security measures.
How are winners decided? ▼
Winners are determined based on trading performance metrics including total returns, risk management, and consistency. Our expert jury panel will evaluate all participants.
